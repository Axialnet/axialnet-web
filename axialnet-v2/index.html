METHODOLOGY

Why domain-specific
AI wins.

General-purpose LLMs hallucinate under domain-specific pressure. A geotechnical engineer asking about soil liquefaction risk cannot afford a confident wrong answer.

We build narrow, deep, rigorously validated models — designed for the expert who already knows the domain and needs AI that keeps up.

Follow Our Research
1

Domain Immersion

Study actual workflows, real reports, practitioner vocabulary. Not tokenized text — lived domain knowledge.

2

Synthetic Data Generation

3

Parameter-Efficient Fine-tuning

QLoRA on 7B models. Deployable on-premise without GPU clusters. Designed for field use.

4

Hybrid Retrieval

BM25 + dense vector search with Reciprocal Rank Fusion. Structured context from unstructured reports.
